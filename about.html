About Us

We are EB Suite.

An energetic team of techs & designers, multi-skilled experts and curious IT specialists who are always up for a challenge and learning as fast as the industry is changing.

Our Story

Built to create, designed to deliver.

Founded in 2014, EB Suite was born from a simple belief: great digital products happen when technology and creativity work hand in hand. We started as a small team of developers and designers passionate about crafting beautiful, functional websites and apps — and we've grown into a full-stack digital studio serving clients across Hong Kong, Mainland China, and beyond.

Over the past decade, we've completed 200+ projects for clients ranging from ambitious startups to established enterprises. Our cross-functional team brings together expertise in strategy, design, development, and marketing — allowing us to handle everything from a single landing page to a full-scale digital transformation.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Every project is a new challenge, and we approach each one with curiosity, craft, and a commitment to delivering results that exceed expectations.
